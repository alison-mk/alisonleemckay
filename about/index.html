About Me

During the height of Harry Potter mania, I once spent an entire childhood summer indoors building a Harry Potter fan website.

My first website's impact was limited — reaching more than my parents but less than my entire middle school — but contributing to the internet was empowering and exciting as an 11 year old.

Flash forward 12 years, to just after college: I began exploring the internet world again, and I found it full of passionate and creative humans. Even more, the internet and design industry moves at lightning speed. Technology changes rapidly, new challenges and trends abound, and improvements take hold quickly.

Intrigued by the possibilities of the web and inspired by the people and projects I encountered, I picked up an internship at Conflare in Seattle's Capitol Hill neighborhood to learn the ropes, moved on to Efelle Creative for 3.5 years, and spent the last 2.5 years with the ethically- and socially-minded design studio Civilization.
